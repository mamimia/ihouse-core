6. Concierge Profile
CONCIERGE CONCEPT — Design Thinking

Service-First Philosophy: Concierge is fundamentally different from operations. The concierge is the human face of hospitality — answering messages, making recommendations, arranging services, proactively assisting guests. Operations handles systems, maintenance, supply chains.

Conversation-First UI: The primary interface is chat. Every screen connects back to guest conversations. Recommendations, arrangements, and escalations flow from understanding what guests need. Local Guide is the knowledge base that informs recommendations.

Escalation Boundary: When a guest request becomes operational (AC breaks, supplies needed, schedule changes), concierge escalates to ops. Concierge remains the guest contact; ops solves the problem. This boundary is critical.

Visual Distinction: Warm navy (#1E3A5F) + gold (#C4956A) creates a completely distinct visual identity from operational surfaces. The tone is calm, premium, warm — not operational urgency.

Concierge Vocabulary: Arrange, Recommend, Assist, Coordinate, Serve. Not "assign," "schedule," "manage." The language reinforces hospitality, not operations.
