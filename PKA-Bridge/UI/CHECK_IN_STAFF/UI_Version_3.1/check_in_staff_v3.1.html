Check-In Staff Surface v3.1 (Refined)

Arrival-guided worker experience with strengthened clarity, confidence, and operational polish.

V3.1 Refinements
Day health now shows progress bar + "5 of 8" counter. Arrival cards: property name dominant, guest secondary, cleaner status badges (3px accent). Tighter spacing (16px padding, 10-12px gaps). All labels uppercase + letter-spacing. OCR confidence integrated into display (no float). Photo capture more guided. Settlement policy & identity steps feel more informational/guest-facing.
Operational Confidence
Step indicator refined for clarity. Reduced visual noise — no colored pills, more subtle badges. Summary rows more compact. Field-worker never confused about which arrival they're in, what step they're on, or what to do next. Wizard progression always visible. Timing gates clear. Deposit/settlement expectations surfaced early.
Design Tightening
Spacing: 16px content padding, 10px gaps between cards, 3px left accents (not 4px). Manrope for all display labels (12px, 800 weight). Inter body text (11px, 500). Badges more integrated (smaller, sharper). Confidence badge inline with OCR result. Field-worker tool feel — removed "board" aesthetic, strengthened field-ops presentation.
Real Product Behavior
Conditional wizard preserved (meter, deposit, identity). OCR with confidence scoring. Timing gate on acknowledge. Settlement policy preview at top of wizard. QR delivery with SMS/email dispatch. Multi-step document capture (passport/ID/license tabs). All behaviors from V3 retained; visual refinement only.
