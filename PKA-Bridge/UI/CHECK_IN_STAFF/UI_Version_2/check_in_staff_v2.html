Check-In Staff UI v2

Interactive prototype showing 4 key screens • Domaniqo Property Management

Role Personality
Arrival-focused, guided, controlled. Check-in staff work is time-driven and guest-facing. Cards show guest names prominently. The vocabulary is warm: "Welcome", "Arrive", "Ready" — not "Task" or "Process".
Urgency System
Countdown to guest arrival time. Same 4 states (Safe → Approaching → Imminent → Overdue) but triggered by scheduled check-in time, not window.
Distinct from Check-Out
Check-in uses ARRIVAL badge (moss green #334036), not DEPARTURE (copper). Flow steps are guest-welcoming oriented: Verify → Keys → Walkthrough → Welcome. No damage inspection.
What Stayed Calm
History and Profile have no urgency. The flow screen uses copper only for current-step dot. The checklist is calm and methodical.
