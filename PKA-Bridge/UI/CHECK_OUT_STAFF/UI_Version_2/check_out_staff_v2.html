Check-Out Staff — Operational UI Direction v2
Domaniqo · April 2026 · 4 screens: Departure Queue · Condition Check · Deposit Decision · Profile
Role Personality Confirmatory, closure-driven. Check-out staff verify departure, inspect condition, and close bookings. The vocabulary is methodical: "Inspect", "Verify", "Close" — not "Welcome" or "Prepare". Condition findings drive the deposit decision.
Urgency System Countdown to scheduled checkout time. Same 4 states. But check-out adds a turnover chain alert: if a late departure risks the next check-in, the card shows the dependency. This is unique to checkout.
Distinct from Check-In Check-out uses Signal Copper (#B56E45) not Deep Moss. The flow is: Verify Departure → Condition Check → Deposit Decision → Close. No guest greeting, no key handover, no walkthrough. Instead: room inspection, damage classification, deposit action.
What Stayed Calm The Deposit Decision screen is deliberate and calm — it's a judgment call, not an emergency. Profile shows performance metrics instead of connection status. History uses copper left borders.
