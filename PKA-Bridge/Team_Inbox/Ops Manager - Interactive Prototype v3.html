S01 · Hub
Operational Hub
Role Philosophy
Oversight, not execution. The UI gives the manager a live picture — not a task queue to clear.
Alert-First Design
Exceptions come first. Calm operations stay quiet. Urgency is communicated through color and animation — not volume.
Stream-First Thinking
The manager thinks in Check-In · Check-Out · Cleaner · Maintenance — four live operational flows, not one flat task list.
Intervene, Not Execute
Manager verbs: Reassign · Escalate · Call · Coordinate · Monitor. Not: Start / Complete / Mark Done.
Booking-Linked Reality
Operations are chained to real bookings — departures, arrivals, exceptions. The chain is always visible.
