Cleaner — Operational UI Direction
Domaniqo · March 2026 · 4 screens: Home · Active Clean · Room Detail + Photo · Property Ready
Role personality
Photo-first, room-by-room. Cleaner work is spatial (one room at a time) and evidence-based (photos prove readiness). Task cards show a photo badge with required count. Progress is tracked in room dots, not step numbers. The vocabulary is clean: "Clean", "Done", "Ready" — not "Task" or "Step".
Urgency system
Window-based countdown — cleaners work within a check-in window (e.g., 12:00–16:00), not a precise departure time. Countdown shows time until window closes. Same 4 states as Check-in/out but triggered by window deadline, not exact guest time.
Distinct from Check-In/Out
Cleaner cards have room dots (spatial progress), a photo count badge (evidence requirement), and a window label instead of guest name. The active screen shows a room list not a condition checklist. Completion is "Property Ready" — a satisfying visual payoff.
What stayed calm
Room detail screen is calm and focused — the cleaner is mid-task, not under threat. Photo slots are clear and inviting, not demanding. The Property Ready screen is the most calm surface: large green check, confirmation summary, no urgency whatsoever.
