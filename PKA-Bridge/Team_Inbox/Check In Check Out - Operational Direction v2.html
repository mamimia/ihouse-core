Check-In & Check-Out — Operational Direction v2
Domaniqo · March 2026 · 4 screens: Home · Active Flow · History · Profile
What was refined
Screen 2 is new — active step flow was missing from v1. The execution wizard now appears inline. Type separation is stronger: Deep Moss = arrivals, Signal Copper = departures, carried through card borders, badges, and active flow header. Profile now shows account linking (Google + password).
Urgency system
4 states on every task card. Safe (white/gray) → Approaching (amber, >30 min) → Imminent (copper, <30 min, pulsing) → Overdue (red, blinking countdown). Day Health strip at top shows live stream counts. Overdue dot blinks independently.
Card scan order
Worker reads left to right: type badge (ARRIVAL/DEPARTURE) → countdown pill → nav icon → property name → guest + time → CTA. All critical info in under 2 seconds. No label clutter.
What stayed calm
History and Profile have no urgency animation. They are reporting surfaces, not action surfaces. The active flow screen uses copper only for the current-step indicator — the checklist itself is calm and task-focused.
